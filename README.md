# 🚚 Supply Chain Control Tower: Risk, Inventory & Logistics Intelligence

## 📌 Project Overview

Modern supply chains face challenges such as inventory imbalance, shipment delays, demand volatility, and operational inefficiencies. This project analyzes end-to-end supply chain operations to identify key business risks, optimize inventory management, improve logistics performance, and support data-driven decision-making.

Using Python, Power BI, and exploratory analytics, the project delivers actionable insights across inventory, fulfillment, shipment performance, and supply chain risk monitoring.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze supply chain performance across inventory, logistics, and fulfillment operations.
* Identify causes of revenue decline and operational inefficiencies.
* Monitor inventory levels and storage costs.
* Evaluate shipment delays and logistics performance.
* Segment inventory using ABC-XYZ analysis.
* Provide recommendations to improve supply chain resilience.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Business Performance

* Generated approximately $5.5M in Net Sales.
* Achieved approximately $4M in Profit.
* Maintained a Profit Margin of approximately 72%.

### Inventory Management

* Excess inventory levels exceeded demand by up to 30% in certain periods.
* High-value inventory segments contributed the majority of revenue.

### Logistics Performance

* Average late shipment rate remained close to 40%.
* Shipment delays were observed across multiple markets and product categories.

### Root Cause Analysis

* Revenue decline was primarily associated with supply-side disruptions.
* Reduced availability of historically high-performing product segments significantly impacted overall performance.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### Inventory Optimization

* Implement demand forecasting.
* Reduce excess inventory levels.
* Define product-specific reorder points.

### Supply Chain Risk Management

* Diversify supplier networks.
* Monitor inventory volatility.
* Improve visibility across supply chain operations.

### Logistics Improvement

* Optimize delivery routes.
* Improve fulfillment efficiency.
* Collaborate with regional logistics partners.
